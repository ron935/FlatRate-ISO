Five Steps to Residual Income

1

Apply Online — Free, Fast, No Experience Required

Fill out our partner application form. Tell us your background, which verticals interest you, and whether you have an existing portfolio. We respond within one business day. There are no application fees, no background hurdles, and no experience requirements — we train everyone from scratch.

2

Get FRP Certified — 3 to 5 Days, Self-Paced

Once approved, you'll get access to FRP's certification curriculum. It covers everything: how interchange pricing works, how to read a merchant statement, how to present FRP's value proposition, vertical-specific sales tactics for Government, Auto, and Restaurant, and how to handle every objection. Most agents complete certification in 3–5 days.

Receive Your Full Agent Kit

After certification, you'll receive access to every tool in the FlatRate ISO arsenal: the statement analyzer, proposal generator, mobile app login, co-branded marketing materials, and your vertical-specific sales playbooks. You also get a dedicated onboarding call with an FRP agent specialist who walks you through your first target list.

Close Your First Accounts

Hit the field with confidence. Pull a statement, run the analysis, show the savings — and let the numbers do the talking. Submit new merchant applications directly through the portal. Our underwriting team turns most merchants around in 24–48 hours.
